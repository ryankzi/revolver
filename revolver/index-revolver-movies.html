Pistols in Movies

Colt M1911 - really famous, represents manliness and harshness. In movies like "Casablanca", "Pulp Ficttion" "Last Man Standing" and "The Punisher". Beretta 92 is also like him and in movies like "Lethal weapon" and "Die hard". Mauser C96 - pretty much no World war 2 movies without him, but also in "Star Wars" and "Mad Max". Represends evil. Desert Eagle is iconic long pistol in many franchisers like RoboCop, Commando, Alien vs. Predator, Underworld, The Matrix. Glock - the first plastic austrian pistol, that is most sold today is in maany movies like Bad Boys 2, Terminator 3, Saw IV, 15 Minutes, 16 Blocks. SIG Sauer P220 - pistol for spy movies like in "Jayson Born" Other guns are Heckler & Koch USP Match and Walther PPK in "Jaymes Bond".
